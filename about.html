About V Pet

Built on love for animals.

A passion-driven pet care business, proudly rooted in Skudai, Johor.

Our Story

From a small studio to a full pet care centre.

V Pet Grooming & Pet Hotel Services was founded with one simple belief — every pet deserves to be treated with love, care and respect.

What started as a grooming studio in Skudai has grown into a complete destination for pet owners across Johor, offering grooming, hotel stays, curated products and adoption services.

Our team is made up of genuine animal lovers who go above and beyond for every pet that comes through our doors.
