FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

✂️ Grooming

It depends on your dog's breed, coat condition and the package chosen. A basic wash typically takes 1 to 1.5 hours, while a full groom or premium package can take 2 to 3 hours. We recommend dropping your pet off and heading out — we'll contact you when they're ready.

Yes, appointments are required so we can give every pet the time and attention they deserve. You can easily book a slot by sending us a WhatsApp message at 011-3602 0431. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.

Yes! We groom all breeds, from small dogs like Chihuahuas and Shih Tzus to larger breeds like Golden Retrievers and Huskies. Our groomers are experienced in breed-specific styling and coat care.

Just bring your dog! However, it helps if you also bring:

  • Up-to-date vaccination records (for first-time visits)
  • Any specific styling references or photos
  • Your dog's favourite toy or treat to keep them calm

Absolutely. Our groomers are trained to handle nervous and anxious dogs with patience and a calm approach. We take extra time and never rush the process. Please let us know in advance so we can prepare accordingly.

🏨 Pet Hotel

Check-in is from 10am and check-out is by 7pm, in line with our operating hours (Tuesday to Sunday). If you need an early drop-off or late pick-up, please contact us in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate.

To make your pet's stay as comfortable as possible, please bring:

  • Vaccination records (mandatory)
  • Your pet's regular food (if they have a specific diet)
  • Any medication with clear instructions
  • A familiar toy or blanket to help them settle in

Yes. Our team provides 24/7 supervision throughout your pet's stay. They are never left alone and will have access to fresh water at all times. Your pet's safety and comfort is our top priority.

Yes! VIP package guests receive daily photo updates via WhatsApp. For other packages, you're always welcome to message us for an update anytime — we're happy to let you know how your pet is doing.

For the safety of all pets in our care, vaccination records are mandatory for hotel stays. We require proof of up-to-date vaccination for rabies, distemper and parvovirus at minimum. Please bring the vaccination booklet on check-in.

💬 General

We are open Tuesday to Sunday, from 10am to 7pm. We are closed on Mondays. Public holiday availability may vary — please check with us via WhatsApp to confirm.

We are located at 83, Jalan Seri Impian 1, Taman Impian Emas, 83100 Skudai, Johor. You can tap the "Get Directions" button on any page of this website to open Google Maps and navigate directly to us.

The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at 011-3602 0431. Just let us know your dog's name, breed, the service you'd like and your preferred date and time. We'll confirm your slot as soon as possible.

We accept cash and online transfers (DuitNow / bank transfer). Payment is typically made upon completion of the service or at check-out for hotel stays.

We occasionally run promotions for returning customers and special occasions. The best way to stay updated is to follow us on social media or ask us directly via WhatsApp. Loyal customers are always appreciated!
